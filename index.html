About

The maths that make AI think.

I'm a Machine Learning developer pursuing B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence at RV College of Engineering, Bangalore. I'm passionate about the mathematics inside large language models: how attention composes, how transformers represent structure, and what makes reasoning emerge at scale.

On the applied side, I build things: RL-driven drone swarms for search and rescue, AI medical examiners with 3D avatars, multi-agent frameworks for orchestrating heterogeneous LLMs, and real-time multiplayer games.

As a Samsung Research Intern, I fine-tuned LLMs for structured information extraction from conversations using the 4W framework. At Verint, I worked on SDE projects involving AI/ML, GenAI, and automation.
