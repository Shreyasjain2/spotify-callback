About

Passionate about the maths that make AI think.

I'm a Machine Learning developer at RV College of Engineering, Bangalore — currently studying how intelligence emerges from numbers, gradients, and reward signals.

My particular obsession is mathematics inside LLMs: how attention composes, how transformers represent structure, and what makes reasoning emerge at scale. On the other side, I'm drawn to Reinforcement Learning — training agents to act well in worlds they have to discover themselves.

Outside of research, I build things: drones that search and rescue, bots that speak medicine, games that challenge you, and frameworks that let agents collaborate.
